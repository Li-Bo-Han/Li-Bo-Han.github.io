Computer Science and Technology
(Joint Training Program with Accounting Major, University of International Business and Economics)

A joint training program by Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT) and University of International Business and Economics (UIBE), where students are registered at BUPT as full-time undergraduate student.

Relying on the Computer Science and Technology major of BUPT and the Accounting major of UIBE, this program fully integrate the advantages of both universities in these majors, aiming to cultivate high-quality, multi-skilled, and innovative talents who are urgently needed in the digital economy era and are proficient in both computer science and accounting. Students are expected to become computer science and technology professionals and accounting talents who can adapt to the technological revolution and industrial transformation, in order to better serve the national strategy and social economic development needs.

Computer Science and Technology is a key and advantageous major of BUPT, and it is also one of the first national-level characteristic majors. In 2019, this major was selected as a national first-class undergraduate program construction project, making it one of the top universities in China for computer science majors. The Accounting major of the International Business School of UIBE has been admitting students since 1985 and was selected as a national first-class undergraduate program construction project in 2019.

Degrees

A 4-year program, awarding a Bachelor of Engineering degree.

In the first two semesters, students undergo general computer training. In the second semester, they make their major choices based on their preferences. Starting from the third semester, they will be trained according to their specific majors.
